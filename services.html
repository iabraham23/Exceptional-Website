How We Execute

A Clear System for Athlete
Financial Decision-Making

This is not a menu of disconnected services. It is one coordinated process built to handle contract cash flow, tax complexity, and long-term planning decisions.

We combine planning coordination and ongoing oversight so major decisions stay aligned. Each area below connects to the same plan and review cadence.

01

Investment Management

Money management support tied to your timeline, liquidity needs, and real-world decision pressure.

View full scope

Your financial decisions should follow a clear process, not headlines. We help structure accounts, cash flow, and oversight with consistent reviews and clear priorities. When this matters most: after your first large contract or endorsement cycle, when cash flow jumps and pressure to "do something" is high.

  • Account setup aligned to career stage and time horizon
  • Ongoing oversight with clear decision checkpoints
  • Liquidity planning integrated into major decisions
  • Review cadence aligned to season and career milestones
02

Multi-State Tax Planning

Planning coordination for multi-state filings, estimated payments, and endorsement/NIL income.

View full scope

Athletes often face tax obligations across multiple states and income types. We coordinate with your CPA so estimated payments, reserve targets, and filing plans are addressed before deadlines create unnecessary penalties and stress. When this matters most: NIL income and rookie years, when withholding is limited and first-year tax surprises are common.

  • Multi-state planning framework coordinated with your CPA
  • Estimated payment and reserve schedule planning
  • Coordination on timing decisions with tax impact
  • Residency and cash flow considerations for planning
  • NIL and endorsement income planning support
03

Estate & Trust Planning

Estate and trust planning coordination to keep legacy decisions aligned with your broader plan.

View full scope

As wealth grows, ownership and beneficiary decisions become more consequential. We coordinate with your estate attorney so account setup, beneficiary alignment, and long-term transfer priorities work together in practice. When this matters most: after a contract jump, marriage, children, or major asset purchases.

  • Coordination for trust and will implementation planning
  • Beneficiary review and account titling alignment
  • Family governance and decision-role planning support
  • Coordination with estate attorneys and tax professionals
  • Legacy priorities integrated into your overall plan
04

Risk Management & Insurance

Risk planning for income disruption, liability exposure, and household continuity.

View full scope

Athlete careers can change quickly, so risk planning cannot be an afterthought. We help structure insurance and protection priorities around realistic downside scenarios so your plan remains functional during disruption. When this matters most: before signing long-term commitments tied to projected future income.

  • Disability and loss-of-income coverage review support
  • Life insurance framework tied to household obligations
  • Liability and umbrella coverage planning priorities
  • Asset protection coordination with legal professionals
  • Periodic policy review and gap identification
05

Contract & Deal Analysis

Financial review support for contracts, endorsements, and non-cash compensation tradeoffs.

View full scope

Headline numbers rarely tell the whole story. We help evaluate cash timing, tax impact, concentration risk, and downside scenarios so decisions fit your broader plan. When this matters most: first contract and early endorsement opportunities where speed and pressure can lead to avoidable mistakes.

  • Contract cash flow and term tradeoff analysis support
  • Endorsement and equity compensation scenario review
  • NIL agreement economics and planning implications
  • Outside opportunity diligence from a planning perspective
  • Signing bonus reserve and spending framework planning
06

Retirement & Career Transition

Planning for income transition, withdrawal planning, and post-career decision continuity.

View full scope

Career transition works better when planned before the final season. We map income changes, spending requirements, and withdrawal needs so your next phase starts with structure instead of uncertainty. When this matters most: mid-career, when you still have flexibility to shape outcomes before income shifts.

  • Retirement income and withdrawal framework planning
  • League benefit and pension decision support
  • Post-career venture evaluation from a risk lens
  • Lifestyle and cash flow reset planning
  • Transition timeline planning with defined checkpoints
07

Philanthropy & Charitable Strategy

Structured giving plan aligned to values, cash flow, and long-term sustainability.

View full scope

Many athletes want to give early and often, but ad-hoc giving can create cash flow and planning conflicts. We help structure a repeatable approach so generosity remains sustainable as your income changes. When this matters most: after first major income spikes and during seasons with variable endorsement cash flow.

  • Giving structure selection support (including DAF/foundation options)
  • Cash flow-aware contribution planning
  • Coordination with tax professionals on giving plans
  • Mission and governance framework development support
  • Long-term giving cadence and review planning
08

Financial Literacy & Education

Practical education so you can evaluate financial decisions with more confidence.

View full scope

A strong plan works best when you understand it. We teach the core concepts behind your decisions so you can spot bad advice, ask better questions, and make decisions faster under pressure. When this matters most: NIL and rookie years, when your financial team is forming and stakes rise quickly.

  • One-on-one education sessions tied to active decisions
  • Cash flow and reserve planning fundamentals
  • Statement and reporting interpretation support
  • Red-flag training for financial exploitation risk
  • Planning education resources for family/support teams

How We Execute

A Clear System for Complex
Financial Decision-Making

This is not a menu of disconnected services. It is one coordinated process built to handle variable income, tax complexity, and long-term planning decisions.

We combine planning coordination and ongoing oversight so major decisions stay aligned. Each area below connects to the same plan and review cadence.

01

Foundation & Cash Flow

The first priority is simple: know what is coming in, what needs to be reserved, and what decisions can wait.

Investment Management

Build account structure and liquidity rules around your career timeline, not short-term noise.

  • Account setup by horizon and cash needs
  • Decision checkpoints during season and off-season

Multi-State Tax Planning

Coordinate with your CPA so reserve targets and estimated payments are handled before pressure builds.

  • Multi-state payment and filing coordination
  • Project, contract, and endorsement income reserve planning

02

Protection & Legacy

As your earnings accelerate, legal structure and risk coverage need to catch up quickly.

Estate & Trust Planning

Align ownership, beneficiaries, and trust structure with your legal team so decisions hold up over time.

  • Beneficiary and account titling alignment
  • Coordination with estate counsel and tax professionals

Risk Management & Insurance

Review core protection gaps so your plan can absorb injury, liability, or income disruption scenarios.

  • Coverage review for disability, life, and umbrella needs
  • Policy updates as contracts and obligations change

03

Contracts & Career

Big money decisions happen fast. We slow down the financial implications so you can choose clearly.

Contract & Deal Analysis

Pressure-test deals beyond the headline number, including tax timing, concentration, and downside risk.

  • Cash timing and reserve impact review
  • Deal, equity, and endorsement scenario analysis

Retirement & Career Transition

Plan the shift from active income to long-term income before the final season arrives.

  • Transition cash flow and withdrawal framework
  • Post-career timeline with defined checkpoints

04

Impact & Education

Good plans stick when giving decisions and day-to-day financial judgment are both sustainable.

Philanthropy & Charitable Strategy

Create a giving structure that matches your values and remains workable through income swings.

  • Giving structure and cadence planning
  • Coordination with tax team on contribution strategy

Financial Literacy & Education

Build confidence around core decisions so you can identify bad advice early and act with more control.

  • Decision-focused education sessions
  • Practical red-flag training for exploitation risk

What We Do

Comprehensive Wealth Management,
Built for Athletes

Your financial life is anything but ordinary. Between compressed earning windows, multi-state obligations, and the public spotlight, you need advisors who understand every dimension of your world.

Most wealth management firms bolt on athlete services as an afterthought. We built ours from the ground up — every strategy, every process, every conversation is designed around the realities professional athletes face. Here's how we help.

01

Investment Management

Your earning window may be short, but your investment horizon extends decades beyond the final whistle. We build diversified portfolios grounded in disciplined, research-driven analysis — identifying opportunities where the market has mispriced value and positioning your capital for long-term, sustainable growth.

  • Customized portfolio construction based on your career timeline and risk profile
  • Value-oriented approach focused on long-term wealth creation
  • Access to alternative investments including private equity and real estate
  • Regular portfolio reviews aligned to your season and career milestones
02

Multi-State Tax Planning

Professional athletes face one of the most complex tax situations of any profession. You may owe income tax in every state — and sometimes city — where you play, practice, or earn endorsement income. The "jock tax" is real, and without proactive planning, it can silently erode your wealth.

  • Comprehensive multi-state and multi-jurisdiction tax strategy
  • Proactive tax projection and estimated payment scheduling
  • Coordination with CPAs to maximize deductions and credits
  • Residency planning to optimize your overall tax position
  • Endorsement and NIL income tax structuring
03

Estate & Trust Planning

Generational wealth doesn't happen by accident. It requires intentional structures — trusts, wills, beneficiary designations, and governance frameworks — that protect what you've built and ensure it passes to the people and causes you care about most.

  • Revocable and irrevocable trust design and implementation
  • Will preparation and beneficiary coordination
  • Family governance structures for multi-generational wealth
  • Charitable trust and foundation planning
  • Coordination with estate attorneys and tax professionals
04

Risk Management & Insurance

One injury can change everything. Athletes carry a level of physical and financial risk that few other professions share. We help you build a protection framework that covers the what-ifs — so your family and your future aren't riding on a single play.

  • Disability insurance and loss-of-value coverage analysis
  • Life insurance planning tailored to high-income, short-career profiles
  • Liability protection and umbrella policy structuring
  • Asset protection strategies for high-profile individuals
  • Insurance policy audit and optimization
05

Contract & Deal Analysis

Not every deal is a good deal. Whether it's your first professional contract, an endorsement opportunity, or an equity stake in a startup, the financial terms matter as much as the headline number. We analyze the details so you sign with confidence.

  • Financial term analysis for player contracts and extensions
  • Endorsement deal evaluation including equity compensation review
  • NIL contract assessment for college athletes
  • Business venture and investment opportunity due diligence
  • Signing bonus structuring and cash flow planning
06

Retirement & Career Transition

The transition out of professional sports is one of the most significant financial and personal shifts you'll ever experience. Average career lengths range from just 3 to 6 years depending on the sport — meaning you could retire in your early 30s with decades of life ahead. We start planning years before your final game.

  • Retirement income modeling and sustainable withdrawal strategies
  • League pension and benefit optimization
  • Post-career business and entrepreneurship evaluation
  • Lifestyle adjustment planning and cash flow restructuring
  • Second-career support including broadcasting, coaching, and venture roles
07

Philanthropy & Charitable Strategy

Many athletes want to give back — to their communities, their schools, their causes. But unstructured giving can be inefficient and unsustainable. We help you design a philanthropic strategy that maximizes impact while providing meaningful tax benefits.

  • Charitable foundation setup and management guidance
  • Donor-advised fund strategy and implementation
  • Tax-efficient giving strategies tied to your income peaks
  • Cause identification and philanthropic mission development
  • Partnership with community organizations and nonprofits
08

Financial Literacy & Education

Knowledge is the ultimate edge. Too many athletes enter the professional world without the financial literacy to match their talent. We believe in empowering you to understand every decision — not just trust that someone else is handling it.

  • One-on-one financial literacy coaching sessions
  • Budgeting and cash flow management fundamentals
  • Understanding investment statements and performance reports
  • Red flag awareness: how to identify financial exploitation
  • Resources for family members and support networks

Every financial situation is different. Schedule a confidential conversation and we'll help you identify which services matter most for where you are right now.

Quick Context

Why Athlete Planning Needs Specialized Execution

Compressed Earning Window

Many athletes earn most of their lifetime income over a short career period.

Multi-State Tax Complexity

Playing, training, and endorsement income can create filing obligations across jurisdictions.

NIL and 1099 Income

Income often arrives without withholding, which increases planning and reserve requirements.

Long Post-Career Horizon

Financial decisions must support decades after active competition ends.

Jock Tax and Multi-State Filings Tax

What it is: Income may be apportioned across states based on where games and duties happen.

Why it matters: Without planning, estimated taxes and withholding can create avoidable penalties and liquidity stress.

How we help: We coordinate strategy and timing with your CPA so tax obligations are anticipated and organized.

  • Common mistake: waiting until year-end to map filing exposure.
  • Common mistake: assuming one-state withholding solves everything.
NIL Income and Quarterly Tax Planning NIL

What it is: NIL and brand income often arrives as 1099 compensation with no tax withheld.

Why it matters: Irregular cash inflows can lead to over-spending before tax liabilities are funded.

How we help: We build reserve policies and payment cadences aligned to your income and filing calendar.

  • Common mistake: treating gross deal value as spendable cash.
  • Common mistake: ignoring quarterly estimate deadlines.
Signing Bonus and Cash Reserve Strategy Cash Flow

What it is: Large one-time deposits require structured allocation across taxes, liquidity, and long-term assets.

Why it matters: Early misallocation can reduce flexibility and increase pressure later.

How we help: We set staged allocation rules that protect near-term liquidity and long-term plan integrity.

  • Common mistake: making major commitments before reserve funding.
  • Common mistake: no defined short-term liquidity target.
Endorsement and 1099 Income Structuring Business

What it is: Endorsement income can involve variable timing, expenses, and entity-level decisions.

Why it matters: Poor documentation and ad hoc structure can increase tax friction and execution risk.

How we help: We coordinate with legal and tax advisors so contracts and income flows stay decision-ready.

  • Common mistake: signing deals without modeling net-after-tax outcomes.
  • Common mistake: no standardized documentation process.
Injury Risk, Protection, and Career Volatility Risk

What it is: Sports careers carry higher interruption risk than most professions.

Why it matters: A sudden change in availability or role can impact both income and long-term planning assumptions.

How we help: We integrate risk mitigation and contingency planning into your full financial strategy.

  • Common mistake: protection planning done in isolation from overall financial plan.
  • Common mistake: no downside scenario cash-flow model.
Family Support Boundaries and Governance Governance

What it is: Financial requests from family and friends can increase quickly as income rises.

Why it matters: Without structure, generosity can conflict with personal goals and long-term sustainability.

How we help: We help define clear support frameworks and decision rules aligned with your priorities.

  • Common mistake: major ongoing commitments without boundaries.
  • Common mistake: no process for evaluating requests consistently.
Post-Career Transition Planning Long-Term

What it is: Transitioning from competition changes income sources, schedule, and planning priorities.

Why it matters: Plans built only for peak years can underperform after retirement.

How we help: We model sustainable income and align investments, tax strategy, and spending to post-career life.

  • Common mistake: delaying transition planning until final seasons.
  • Common mistake: lifestyle assumptions not stress-tested for retirement horizon.

Educational information only. Planning and tax implementation should be coordinated with your CPA, attorney, and advisory team.

Our Approach

Discipline Over Drama.
Value Over Hype.

Markets are noisy. Careers are short. The financial strategies that work for athletes aren't built on chasing headlines — they're built on conviction, research, and a long-term view that extends far beyond the final game.

The Reality

Why Athletes Need a Different Playbook

The average NFL career lasts 3.3 years. MLB players average about 5.6. Even top-tier athletes who play for a decade or more will earn the vast majority of their lifetime income before age 35.

That's the fundamental challenge: you need to build a financial plan that turns a compressed, high-intensity earning window into wealth that sustains you, your family, and your legacy for 50+ years after the last game.

Add in multi-state taxes, public visibility, intense pressure to spend, and an industry full of people eager to profit from your success — and it becomes clear why standard wealth management doesn't cut it.

We built our approach specifically for this reality.

Investment Philosophy

How We Invest Your Money

Our investment approach is grounded in a principle we've followed for over two decades: markets misprice assets, and disciplined investors can capitalize on that.

01

Value-Driven Selection

Markets tend to push prices to extremes — both up and down. We identify assets whose prices don't reflect their true worth. When a company is priced as if it will never recover, or a growth stock is valued as if nothing could ever go wrong, those are opportunities.

Buying when expectations are low doesn't just create opportunity for gain — it naturally reduces risk. This disciplined, contrarian approach has guided our portfolio decisions for over 25 years.

02

Diversified Portfolio Construction

No single investment should make or break your financial future. We build diversified portfolios across asset classes — public equities, fixed income, real estate, and selectively, alternative investments — tailored to your specific timeline and risk tolerance.

For athletes, this means portfolios designed to weather the volatility of both markets and careers. Your portfolio should work for you whether you're in your peak earning years or twenty years into retirement.

03

Tax-Efficient Execution

For an athlete earning across multiple states, every basis point of tax efficiency matters. We integrate tax considerations into every investment decision — from asset location strategies to tax-loss harvesting to the timing of gains and distributions.

The goal isn't just strong returns — it's strong after-tax returns. That distinction can mean millions over the course of a career and retirement.

04

Risk Management First

We wrap our investment philosophy into a disciplined implementation process that actively monitors and controls macro risks. Markets will be volatile — your plan shouldn't be.

We stress-test portfolios against multiple economic scenarios, maintain appropriate liquidity buffers, and ensure you're never overexposed to a single position, sector, or outcome.

The Process

How We Work Together

Getting started is straightforward. Here's what to expect when you partner with Exceptional Investing.

1

Discovery

We start with a confidential conversation about your career, your family, your goals, and your concerns. No sales pitch — just listening. We want to understand your full financial picture, who else is involved in your decision-making, and what matters most to you.

2

Strategy & Planning

Based on what we learn, we build a comprehensive written plan covering cash flow, taxes, investments, insurance, and estate considerations. This isn't a template — it's a customized blueprint designed around your specific career timeline and life goals.

3

Implementation

We open accounts, position your portfolio, coordinate with your CPA and attorney, and set up the structures your plan calls for. We also establish a seasonal calendar so key financial actions align with your playing schedule — not an arbitrary fiscal year.

4

Ongoing Partnership

Your plan isn't a document that sits in a drawer. We conduct regular reviews, proactively adjust for life changes, monitor your portfolio daily, and remain available whenever you need us. As your career evolves, your plan evolves with it.

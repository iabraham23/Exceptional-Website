The Reality

Why Athletes Need a Different Playbook

The average NFL career lasts 3.3 years. MLB players average about 5.6. Even top-tier athletes who play for a decade or more will earn the vast majority of their lifetime income before age 35.

That's the fundamental challenge: you need to build a financial plan that turns a compressed, high-intensity earning window into wealth that sustains you, your family, and your legacy for 50+ years after the last game.

Add in multi-state taxes, public visibility, intense pressure to spend, and an industry full of people eager to profit from your success — and it becomes clear why standard wealth management doesn't cut it.

We built our approach specifically for this reality.

The Exceptional Planning Framework

We use one coordinated framework across your key financial decisions so priorities stay clear and nothing important gets missed.

The Process

How We Work Together

Getting started is straightforward. Here's what to expect when you partner with Exceptional Wealth Group.

1

Discovery

We start with a confidential conversation about your career, your family, your goals, and your concerns. No sales pitch — just listening. We want to understand your full financial picture, who else is involved in your decision-making, and what matters most to you.

2

Plan Design

Based on what we learn, we build a written plan covering cash flow, taxes, insurance, and long-term priorities. It is tailored to your timeline and decision pressure points.

3

Setup & Coordination

We open accounts, coordinate with your CPA and attorney, and put key structures in place. We also align major financial actions with your playing calendar.

4

Ongoing Support

Your plan is reviewed regularly and adjusted as life changes. As your career evolves, your planning priorities evolve with it.

Decision Standards

How Money Decisions Are Managed

We keep money-management decisions aligned to your goals, timeline, and tax context, with clear communication at each step.

01

Clear Role for Every Account

Each account and pool of money should have a clear purpose tied to your life and career stage.

That clarity helps reduce random decisions and keeps priorities consistent when new opportunities appear.

02

Diversification and Concentration Control

No single position or decision should determine your long-term outcome. We manage concentration risk and keep exposures balanced.

For athletes, this supports stability through both career volatility and market volatility.

03

Tax-Aware Coordination

Tax impact matters, especially with multi-state income. We coordinate planning decisions with your CPA before key deadlines.

The goal is fewer surprises and cleaner execution across your full plan.

04

Ongoing Oversight

Your plan is reviewed regularly and adjusted when your career, family, or goals change.

You get clear updates on what changed, why it changed, and what to focus on next.

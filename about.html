Who We Are

Built in Portland.
Built for Athletes.

Exceptional Wealth Group was created with a singular focus: to give professional athletes the kind of dedicated, conflict-free wealth management that their careers and families deserve.

Our Story

Exceptional Wealth Group is the athlete-dedicated wealth management practice of CWC Advisors, LLC — a Portland-area investment advisory firm with over two decades of experience managing portfolios for individuals, families, and institutions.

CWC was founded in 2000 with a disciplined, research-driven investment philosophy. Over the years, the team's track record attracted not only high-net-worth individuals but institutional investors as well. That same rigor — the same commitment to identifying real value and managing risk with precision — now powers every strategy we build for professional athletes.

We launched Exceptional Wealth Group because we saw a gap. Too many athletes are served by massive wirehouses that treat sports and entertainment as a marketing category, not a specialty. Too many are sold products instead of given advice. And too many face financial hardship after retirement — not because they didn't earn enough, but because nobody built them a real plan.

We're here to change that. From our home in the Pacific Northwest, we serve athletes across the country with the personal attention of a boutique firm and the investment expertise of a team that's been doing this for over 25 years.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Fiduciary Always

We are legally and ethically bound to act in your best interest. No commissions, no proprietary products, no conflicts. Fee-only, always.

Long-Term Thinking

We don't chase trends or hot stocks. We build portfolios and plans designed to sustain wealth across decades and generations — not just seasons.

Full Transparency

You will always know exactly what you're paying, what you own, and why. No hidden fees, no buried clauses, no financial jargon designed to confuse.

Personal Attention

You're not a number on a spreadsheet. We keep our client base intentionally small so every athlete gets the time, focus, and individualized care they deserve.

Portland Rooted

We're proud to call the Pacific Northwest home. Our local presence means we're accessible, grounded, and connected to the communities that matter to our clients.

Research-Driven

Every investment decision is backed by rigorous analysis. We focus on identifying real value in the market — not following the crowd or chasing hype.

Our Home

Why Portland

Oregon has no state sales tax and offers strategic advantages for athletes establishing residency in the Pacific Northwest. But beyond the tax benefits, Portland is a city that values authenticity, community, and doing things differently.

That ethos is woven into everything we do. We're not a Wall Street firm packaging generic advice under a sports marketing label. We're a team of experienced investors who chose to build something purposeful — right here in the Pacific Northwest — for athletes who want more from their financial team.

Whether you play in Portland or across the country, our doors are always open and our team is always accessible.

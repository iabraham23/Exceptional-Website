The Spotlight Wealth Partners Story

Spotlight Wealth Partners is the sports and entertainment-focused practice of CWC Advisors, LLC. We serve athletes, entertainers, and families who need a tighter process around long-term wealth decisions, deal cash flow, tax coordination, and financial education.

CWC Advisors was founded in 2000 and has served individuals, families, and institutions through multiple market cycles. Spotlight Wealth Partners applies that same planning standard to the unique decision pressure of high-visibility careers.

Many clients are told to "invest for the long term" without an operating plan for what happens now. Our model is different: clear priorities, documented decisions, and ongoing coordination with your broader professional team.

Based in Lake Oswego, Oregon, we serve athletes and entertainers nationwide using the same standard: a simple fee structure, fiduciary advice, transparent communication, and steady follow-through.

How We Work

What Clients Experience

Simple Fee Structure, Fiduciary Standard

You receive advice from a team paid by clients directly through a simple fee structure, not by product commissions. Incentives stay clear, and recommendations are tied to your stated goals.

Long-Horizon Planning Support

We keep major decisions anchored to your timeline and cash needs, so short-term market noise does not dictate long-term outcomes.

Transparent Communication

You get clear scope, plain-language explanations, and education about how your priorities dictate your financial plan.

Ongoing Relationship

You work with people who know your plan and your goals. Review cadence and communication are designed to stay responsive as your career moves quickly.

Nationwide Service, Personal Accountability

You work with a team built to support clients wherever they are, with secure meetings, coordinated planning across states, and consistent follow-through.

Clear Decision Process

Important financial decisions follow a documented, repeatable process with accountability built in.
